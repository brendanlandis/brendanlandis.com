I'm a self-taught designer and front-end developer with over twenty years of experience, living in Queens, NY. I wrote my first lines of HTML in 1997, and started making things professionally in the early 2000s.

Since then, projects I've worked on have ranged from startups to multinationals, from static sites to WCAG-compliant, CMS-driven, internationally available stores with multilingual support, including right-to-left languages.

I like making things that are accessible and welcoming for everyone. That means creating a user experience that's easy, inclusive, and painless — whatever my role in a project may be.

If you have a project you'd like to discuss, please don't hesitate to get in touch.
